NACOSS

WHY NACOSS?

We got your back!

NACOSS means National Association of Computer Science Students. Basically, we are the gap between the lecturers and students. We organise events, give out resources and help with any other information needed. We pay course dues ever session, which is 3000 naira only. The current president of our NACOSS is Samuel Nwabuike.
